Usage Rules

Do's and don'ts for consistent brand application.

✓ Use gradient logo on neutral backgrounds
✗ Don't place on clashing color backgrounds
✓ Use black variant on dark surfaces
✗ Don't reduce logo below minimum size
✓ Maintain clear space around the logo
✗ Don't distort, rotate, or stretch
