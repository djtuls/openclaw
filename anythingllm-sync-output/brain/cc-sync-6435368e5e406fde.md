# Top 10 Reliability Risks - No Graceful Shutdown

**Type:** fact
**Tier:** permanent
**Confidence:** 0.97
**Created:** Mon Feb 09 2026 12:30:43 GMT-0300 (Brasilia Standard Time)

---

The most critical reliability risk is the absence of `process.on('SIGTERM')` or `process.on('SIGINT')` handlers, leading to dropped requests and leaked DB connections upon Docker stop.  Adding ~30 lines for SIGTERM/SIGINT handlers is recommended.
