# Environment variables for channel ingestion toggles

**Type:** fact
**Tier:** permanent
**Confidence:** 0.90
**Created:** Mon Feb 09 2026 12:31:14 GMT-0300 (Brasilia Standard Time)

---

Environment variables `INBOX_CAPTURE_DISCORD_INGEST`, `INBOX_CAPTURE_SLACK_INGEST`, and `INBOX_CAPTURE_NOTION_INGEST` control the activation of each channel ingestion process.
