# Supabase Online Mirror and Vault

**Type:** fact
**Tier:** permanent
**Confidence:** 0.92
**Created:** Mon Feb 09 2026 15:45:15 GMT-0300 (Brasilia Standard Time)

---

A critical component for redundancy and accessibility, Supabase provides an online mirror of the Notion workspace and a secure vault for sensitive information. This allows for continued operation even if the local PostgreSQL database is unavailable. The sync process is automated and runs every 30 minutes, ensuring data consistency.
